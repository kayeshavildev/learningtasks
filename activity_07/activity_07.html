Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

________________________________

Main Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8–12 oz pasta (fettuccine, penne, or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Tuscan-Style Additions

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced

    • Optional Extras

  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh basil or parsley
  • Mushrooms
  • Extra Parmesan for serving

    • ________________________________

    Here’s a simple step-by-step way to make Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta:

  • Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  • Cook seasoned chicken in olive oil until golden and fully cooked, then slice.
  • Sauté garlic and cherry tomatoes in the same pan.
  • Pour in heavy cream and stir.
  • Add Parmesan cheese and mix until smooth.
  • Add spinach and let it wilt.
  • Add pasta and chicken back into the pan and toss together.
  • Serve hot.
